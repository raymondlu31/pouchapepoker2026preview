# Privacy Policy - Pouch Ape Poker 2026

**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## Overview

Pouch Ape Poker 2026 ("the Game") is a probability intuition training game developed by Raymond Lu. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Game.

## Developer Information

- **Developer Name:** Yun Lu
- **Contact Email:** raymondlou2025@outlook.com

## Data Collection

### 1. Information We Collect

The Game does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, including but not limited to:

- Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
- Account information
- Location data
- Device information
- Usage statistics
- Game performance data

### 2. Local Storage Only

All game data, including:

- Game progress
- Scores and statistics
- Game settings and preferences
- Game logs (if enabled)

is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server or third party.

### 3. No Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or social networking services that could collect your personal information.

### 4. No Account Required

You do not need to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to play the Game.

## Data Usage

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted:

- We do not use your personal data for any purpose
- We do not share your personal data with third parties
- We do not analyze user behavior patterns
- We do not send promotional communications

## Data Security

- All game data remains on your device
- No data is transmitted over the internet
- No external databases or servers are used
- Your privacy is maintained at all times

## Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, this concern does not apply to the Game.

## Data Retention

As all data is stored locally on your device:

- Data retention is controlled by your device's storage management
- Data is deleted when you uninstall the Game
- You can delete your game progress at any time by using the Game's settings or by uninstalling the application

## Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected:

- You have no personal data that needs to be accessed, corrected, or deleted
- You have no data that is being shared or processed
- You have complete control over your locally stored game data

## Permissions

The Game may request the following permissions to function properly:

- **File System Access (if applicable):** To save game data locally on your device
- **Audio (if applicable):** To play game sounds and music

These permissions are used solely for the intended functionality of the Game and are not used to collect or transmit personal information.
